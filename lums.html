Lahore University of Management Sciences

LUMS is a not-for-profit university that was incorporated in 1984 and was granted a charter by the Government of Pakistan the following year

Some campuses of lums are as follow

  • Suleman Dawood. School of Business
  • Mushtaq Ahmad Gurmani. School of Humanities and Social Sciences
  • Syed Babar Ali. School of Science and Engineering
  • Shaikh Ahmad Hassan. School of Law
  • Syed Ahsan Ali and Syed Maratib Ali. School of Education
